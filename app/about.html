About

We provide business affairs advice tailored to the needs of artists and businesses in the creative industries. We act as trusted advisers for a range of clients from start-up businesses to world renowned artists and global brands.

Our team has experience of working at top tier law firms whilst also being actively involved in the creative industries. We provide flexible fee arrangements that secure cost certainty and value for our clients. We pride ourselves in being attentive, personable and pragmatic in our approach.

The Team

Aneesh

Aneesh has over 10 years of experience as a lawyer advising clients in the creative industries. He worked at top tier firms including Fieldfisher and Harbottle & Lewis where he advised a range of clients in the music, film, tv, art and technology sectors. Aneesh has acted as General Counsel for a number of start-up and growth companies. He has also worked in-house for some global corporations, including MTV and Amazon Music. Aneesh is now the go-to adviser for a number of highly reputed names in the music industry.

Aneesh is also co-founder of the audiophile music venue Brilliant Corners , and its associated projects GIANT STEPS and Idle Moments.

Fergus

Fergus spent five years working for Lee & Thompson, a media law firm based in London. As an associate solicitor he specialised in dispute resolution, settling disputes and litigating a range of intellectual property and contract claims in the London Courts. 

Fergus was particularly active in the music, film, TV, gaming and marketing and advertising sectors, advising clients on commercial negotiation and contracts, regulatory compliance, risk assessment and clearance issues. Before his time at Lee & Thompson, he worked for Universal Music Group, including its creative and commercial partnerships division, Globe.

Eduardo

Edoardo completed his Master of Laws in Amsterdam and followed up with a diploma in block-chain strategy from Oxford University. He also has worked on Privacy and Compliance for government level organisations in the EU. Edoardo also is part of a DJ duo called “Eternal Love” through which he regularly releases music and tours internationally.
